## Context Engineering
Prompt Engineering = Writing A better Instruction
Context engineering = Giving AI everything A smart human would need to do that job

Ex : Writing An Email To Your 10k Subscribers Inviting Them For This Gen AI WorkShop
### The 5 Layers Of Context
1. Identity Context : Who is the AI Pretending to be? 
2. World Context : What does the AI need to know about the world/domain / your situation, business, audience, products?
3. Task Context : What does the AI need to do?
4. Example Context : What does the AI need to know about the output?
5. Constraints Context : What does the AI need to know about the limitations?
